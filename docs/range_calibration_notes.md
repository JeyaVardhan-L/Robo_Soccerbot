# Transmitter Range Calibration

After measuring receiver output, the TX range was confirmed to be:

- Min ≈ 1200
- Mid ≈ 1500
- Max ≈ 1800

Mapping was updated accordingly.

Improvements observed:

- Full resolution restored
- More predictable steering
- Better low-speed control
- Reduced control compression

Lesson:
Mapping must match real hardware behavior.
Calibration improves feel dramatically.
